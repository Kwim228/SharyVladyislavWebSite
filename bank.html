💸 Finance App Dashboard

This concept explores the design of a modern mobile finance application focused on simplicity and quick access to essential actions. The goal was to create a clean and intuitive interface that allows users to easily manage their balance, send payments, request money, and track recent transactions.

The design prioritizes clarity and usability by organizing information into well-structured sections and highlighting the most important actions directly on the main screen. Large interactive buttons make sending or requesting money fast and effortless, while the transaction history provides a clear overview of recent activity.

Special attention was given to visual hierarchy and minimalism. The interface uses clear typography, simple iconography, and a calm color palette to keep the experience focused and distraction-free while still feeling modern and trustworthy.

Role: UX/UI Design
