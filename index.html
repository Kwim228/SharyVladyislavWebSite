About me

I discovered my passion for design at the age of 14, and since then I’ve been fascinated by creating digital experiences that are both visually appealing and user-friendly. What started as curiosity gradually grew into a deeper interest in how thoughtful design can simplify interactions and improve usability.

Over time, I’ve developed skills in modern design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Canva, and Affinity. My work focuses on UX structure, wireframing, and clean UI design, with a strong emphasis on clarity, usability, and well-organized interfaces.

I’ve also designed websites for charitable organizations, an experience that taught me how to combine aesthetics with purpose and create designs that support meaningful initiatives.
