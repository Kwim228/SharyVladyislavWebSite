FAQ
How long does a project usually take?
Project timelines depend on the scope.
Landing page — typically 1–2 weeks
Business website — around 2–4 weeks
E-commerce design — 3–5 weeks
The timeline may vary depending on feedback speed and project complexity.
What do I receive at the end of the project?
You will receive fully prepared design files including:
Final UI designs for all agreed pages
Responsive layouts (desktop and mobile)
Component-based UI kit (if included in the package)
All files are delivered in a format ready for developers.
Do you also develop the website?
I specialize in UX/UI design only. Development can be handled by your developer or agency using the design files I provide.
Do I need to provide content for the website?
Yes, ideally you should provide the main content such as texts, images, and branding materials. If content is not ready yet, I can help structure the page and use placeholder content during the design phase.
How many revisions are included?
The number of revisions is discussed before the project begins. Minor adjustments are included during the design process, while major structural changes after approval may require additional work.
Can the design be used by developers easily?
Yes. All designs follow a component-based structure, making them clear and convenient for developers to implement.
